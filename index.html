Report Tabs:

  • Main Data: The full dataset with all new flags and calculated columns.
  • Recently Modified Bills: Bills modified in the last 30 days.
  • New Bill Anomalies: Recently created bills with anomalous Usage Z-scores.
  • High Value Anomalies: Bills with unusually high Usage or Usage per Square Foot.
  • Rate Anomalies: Bills with an extreme outlier for the calculated Rate (Cost/Usage).
  • Negative Usage Records: Bills with negative Usage values.
  • Zero Cost Positive Usage: Bills with a zero Cost but positive Usage.
  • Bills After Sale Date: Bills with an End Date after the property's Sold date.
  • Duplicate Records: Rows that are exact duplicates based on key bill details.
  • Gap Records: Bills with a gap in their date history.
  • HCF Mismatch: Bills with a discrepancy between HCF and Usage.
  • No Recent Data Meters: Meters that haven't had a bill in the last 90 days.
  • Zero_Between_Positive: Bills with zero usage between two positive-usage bills.
